Travel

Camp Canada participants have 30 days to travel after camp so make sure you
make the most of it. Is America on your travel bucket list? Well it’s only a hop, skip
and a jump (actually just a plane ride or road trip away) from Canada. Why not join
in one of AmeriCamp‘s legendary NYC or Vegas After Camp Parties? You will meet
even more people as you take in the spectacular sights of ‘the city that never
sleeps’ or party in the ‘city of sin’. You never know what crazy adventures await you
on these trips, so what are you waiting for? Let the fun begin! x
